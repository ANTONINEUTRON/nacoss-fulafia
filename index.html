About us — NACOSS | FULafia

Nigeria Association of Computer Science Students, Federal University of Lafia (NACOSS FULafia) is the NACOSS Chapter for students studying Computer Science in Federal University of Lafia. NACOSS FULafia started out in the 2016/2017 session with Comr. Success Emmanuel Ibrahim as the Pioneer President. And the association in its totality has experience tremendous growth ever since. The association strive to keep up with the aims and objectives of its parent association; NACOSS National. Message from the President

" Welcome to the official website of the NACOSS FULafia Chapter and thank you for taking the time to learn more about us. Every association has its canon. Ours is a community centred around the academic, social and overall wellbeing of our members-students of the department of Computer Science, Federal University of Lafia. Our association is a family of computer science students; regardless of religious, ethnic and ancestral background. As a member of this association, you a guaranteed a ‘WOW’ time on campus as we are committed to getting you the best experience academically, socially and otherwise. Our website contains many resources for you to learn about us and to also reach out to us. I encourage you to visit our departmental building on campus, meet our students and staff, and see for yourself the outstanding opportunities, activities that NACOSS FULafia offers."
— Comr. Zinwota Timothy (President NACOSS FULafia)
