About NACOSS | FULafia

NACOSS FULafia is the NACOSS Chapter for students studying Computer Science in Federal University of Lafia. NACOSS FULafia started out in the 2016/2017 session with Comr. Success Emmanuel Ibrahim as the Pioneer President. And the association in its totality has experience tremendous growth ever since.

The association strive to keep up with the aims and objectives of its parent association; NACOSS National. It does this by providing opportunities for its members to fulfill their in the IT industry both locally and globally. In order to achieve this, NACOSS FULafia organizes seminar and workshops for its members so as to enlighten their IT consciousness and bring them up-to-date with the latest trends in the IT industry.

Furthermore, in order to Foster the spirit of collaboration, NACOSS FULafia usually reach out to other NACOSS Chapters in other institutions by organizing/participating in activities such as inter-chapter football match; tech related activities such as hackathons, meetups, seminars and conferences; and a whole lots more.

About NACOSS | National

NACOSS was founded by groups of students in July 1993 with the backing of Nigerian Computer Society (NCS) as its parent body. It provides avenues for students (in any IT related field) to highlight and champion issues of interest in a coordinated and organized manner.

NACOSS members (NACOSSites) are students studying in tertiary institutions in computer related disciplines including: Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Information Technology, etc.
